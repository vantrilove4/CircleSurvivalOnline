CircleSurvival

How to Play

  • Move the character using your mouse, touch, or joystick (on mobile).
  • Eat gold orbs to gain points.
  • Every 5 levels, a Boss appears. Eating boss orbs shoots at the Boss.
  • Avoid red triangle enemies.
  • Every 3 levels, the number of enemies increases. Every 5 levels, enemies move faster.
  • Power-ups appear every 10 seconds at random positions:
    • Freeze (blue) – freezes all enemies and the boss for 5 seconds.
    • Kill (yellow) – instantly removes one enemy.
    • Super (pink) – turn into Super mode for 3 seconds; touching enemies destroys them.
  • The game ends if you touch an enemy or the Boss.
  • Your score, level, and survival time are displayed when the game is over.
  • You can share your score on Facebook after losing.
